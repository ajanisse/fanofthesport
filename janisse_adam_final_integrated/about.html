About Us

Promo Image


Fan of the Sport was created in 2010 by Marty Hancox in the downtown core of St.Thomas, ON. Since then, we've been able to expand to a new location, offering more and more different sports equipment, novelty, and collectables. We're currently in the process of upgrading our website and providing the ability to shop online for your sports needs. If you're ever in our area, stop by and take a look! It's a great time of year to search for the perfect holiday gifts!
